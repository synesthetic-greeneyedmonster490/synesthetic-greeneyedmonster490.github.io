## 🚀 Understanding CPU parking

Modern processors save power by parking cores when they sit idle. Your computer turns off some cores when demand stays low to save electricity and reduce heat. 

While this design works well for laptops on battery power, it creates delays for desktop users who need maximum performance. ParkControl-2026 prevents your processor from parking cores. This ensures that every core stays ready to handle tasks the moment you start them.
